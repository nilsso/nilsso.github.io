Lambda calculus

Identity function and alpha equivalence:

$$ \lambda x.x,\quad\lambda y.y,\quad\lambda z.z \\ $$

Applying a lambda to an argument:

$$ \begin{align*} (\lambda x.x)y& & (\lambda x.x)(\lambda y.y)& \\ [x:=y]& & [x:=(\lambda y.y)]& \\ y& & (\lambda y.y)& \end{align*} $$
